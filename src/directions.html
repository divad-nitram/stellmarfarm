Directions from Peterborough

1. Take Highway 7 to Little Britain Rd. (You will see a Ford tractor dealership on corner)
2. Turn LEFT and follow Little Britain Rd. to the yellow flashing light which is Elm Tree Rd.
3. Turn LEFT and travel for about 11 kms. You will travel around 3 curves and reach Sand Bar Rd.
4. Turn LEFT and travel to 136 Sand Bar Rd. Stellmar farm is on the left about 1/2 mile on the left.
